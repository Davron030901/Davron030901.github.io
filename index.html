01 About

Engineering for accuracy, built on physics.

I'm an AI engineer who treats data quality as a first principle. Across annotation, model evaluation, and backend integration, I hold production systems to a 98%+ accuracy standard — because a model is only as trustworthy as the pipeline feeding it.

My work spans the full stack of applied AI: training and evaluating CNNs and classical ML models, wiring computer-vision models like YOLO and OpenCV into FastAPI backends for live video, and building LLM tooling with MCP, vLLM, and Qdrant for retrieval.

A degree in Physical Engineering gave me the math and physics foundation I lean on when reasoning about models from the ground up rather than treating them as black boxes.
